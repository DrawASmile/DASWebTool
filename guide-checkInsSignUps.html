Draw a Smile App

Guide: How to validate a user signed up, checked in/out, or signed up to volunteer

First: Go to https://console.firebase.google.com/?pli=1

 

If you are able to reach this page with a project called Draw a Smile, continue to step 2:

·        If you are asked to login, use a google account with access to the Firebase console (such as drawasmilefoundation@gmail.com)

·        If you are already logged into a different account that doesn’t have access to the console (or you don’t see the Draw a Smile project in your list of projects), sign out of your account at the right top corner and login to an account that does have access such as drawasmilefoundation@gmail.com.

 

 

Next: Click on the Draw a Smile project to open it.

3: When expanded the checkedInUsers list will look like this:

 

 

 

·        If you checked in using a Google or Email/Password account,  there should be a new item in the checkedInUsers list with your Firebase UID

            (You can go to the Auth tab to figure out what email address the UID represents. See Guide 1 in this page.)

·        If you checked in using a phone number, the item in the list should just be the phone number that you used.

·        Uid2 is a test user that you should not touch or delete. Just ignore it.

 

 

4: Once you check out, your item in the checkedInUsers list should flash red and get deleted from the list.
